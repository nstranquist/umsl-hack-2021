Guidance to Read Smart and Think Critically

    Reading the article with purpose in your mind
    Putting yourself in the article's context
    Looking for more information toward the problem
    Gathering and analyzing data
    Making questions about what you have read
    Trying to look at thing diffrently
    Seeking for truth in argument and persuasion
    Be yourself! Don't be driven by the biases from the article.
