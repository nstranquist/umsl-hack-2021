Introduction

Everyday new information online is being consumed. Whether or not the information being presented is true is up to you as the reader to interpret. Misinformation or inaccurate information of all kinds, from simple mistakes to conspiracy theories have been around for a while now but they are now being fueled by the ongoing pandemic, through social media or even articles online. This inaccurate information could be hoping to influence political beliefs or personal gain. Our mission is to satisfy our customers looking for accurate information online and prevent them from misleading information.
